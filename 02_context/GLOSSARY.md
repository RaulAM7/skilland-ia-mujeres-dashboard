# GLOSSARY

- Term: SkilLand IA Mujeres
  Definition: Product/project line for women empowerment in AI through programs, platform, itineraries, microcredentials, accompaniment and impact measurement.

- Term: IA Mujeres Dashboard
  Definition: Internal dashboard for monitoring and coordinating the IA Mujeres commercial/institutional funnel.

- Term: Dashboard-owned snapshot
  Definition: Architecture where the dashboard API queries CRM/Twenty and generates the normalized snapshot consumed by the UI.

- Term: Snapshot
  Definition: Compact, normalized JSON representation of funnel state for UI use; not a complete CRM dump.

- Term: CRM/Twenty
  Definition: Deployed CRM environment, based on Twenty CRM, intended as runtime source of truth for opportunities, tasks, stages and related operational data.

- Term: Funnel
  Definition: Commercial/institutional process from exploitable list and first email through replies, meetings, proposals, follow-up, nurturing or closure.

- Term: Opportunity / deal
  Definition: CRM record representing an entity or potential institutional/commercial opportunity in the IA Mujeres funnel.

- Term: Stage
  Definition: Commercial state of an opportunity, such as meeting proposed, meeting scheduled or manual review.

- Term: Outreach status
  Definition: Operational contact state such as pending first email, email sent or follow-up due; not always the same as a commercial stage.

- Term: GWS CLI
  Definition: Google Workspace operational tooling referenced for drafts, sends, message/thread IDs, replies and bounces if available.

- Term: `email_opened`
  Definition: Weak signal; should not be a primary funnel stage or main KPI.

- Term: `reply_received`
  Definition: Strong event that requires human attention and moves a contact out of automatic follow-up.

- Term: `bounce_detected`
  Definition: Blocking event indicating the email/contact may be invalid and needs manual review.

- Term: `meeting_booked`
  Definition: Primary conversion for the first funnel segment.

- Term: Manual review
  Definition: Human data-quality or decision step for ambiguous, duplicated, invalid or blocked records.

- Term: Nurturing
  Definition: Future relationship path for entities that do not respond now but should not be closed permanently.

- Term: ICP
  Definition: Ideal customer/profile segment; in this project may include entity type, territory, target women profile and collaboration line.

- Term: Microcredential
  Definition: Modular credential proving completion or competence for a specific part of the IA Mujeres itinerary.

- Term: FUNDAE
  Definition: Spanish training-funding framework referenced for company training and bonifiable programs.

- Term: RSC / ESG
  Definition: Corporate social responsibility and environmental/social/governance frames for company buyers.
